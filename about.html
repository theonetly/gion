Why we're named giòn

One word. One defining sensation.

The Vietnamese word "giòn," meaning crispy, is one of the defining characteristics of a great bánh mì. From the crackle of a perfectly baked baguette to the contrast of fresh vegetables and savory fillings, that signature crispness is what makes every bite memorable.

We honor that tradition with every sandwich—combining fresh herbs, savory proteins, and our signature giòn bread.

The Origin

From French baguette to Vietnamese icon.

Originally, the term bánh mì referred only to the French baguette introduced to Vietnam during the late 1800s. Over time, Vietnamese cooks transformed it into something uniquely their own—layering it with pickled vegetables, fresh herbs, pâté, and savory proteins.

What began as a simple fusion of French and Vietnamese influences evolved into one of the world's most beloved street foods—a sandwich with a story baked into every layer.
